Disney's Moana

A Polynesian Story of Self-Discovery

"Moana," is an animated feature film by Disney Animation that was released in 2016. It was directed by Ron Clements and John Musker and includes songs written by Lin-Manuel Miranda, Opetaia Foa'i, and Mark Mancina.

The story follows Moana Waialiki who is a spirited and ambitious young girl and daughter of the chief of a Polynesian village. Unlike typical Disney princesses, Moana is depicted as a strong-willed, independent leader. The film stands out for its portrayal of Polynesian culture, which was researched and respectfully depicted. Both critics and audiences expressed appreciation for the approach.
